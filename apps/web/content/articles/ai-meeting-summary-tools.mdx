## What Makes Great AI Meeting Summary Tools?

The best tools don't just dump a transcript on you. Here's what actually matters for summary generation:

- **Structured output that makes sense.** Great summaries organize information into clear sections: key decisions, action items, and discussion themes. You should be able to scan a summary in 30 seconds and know exactly what happened.
- **Customization for different meeting types.** Sales calls need different structures than customer interviews or team standups. The ability to tailor summary formats to your specific use case is crucial.
- **Speed matters.** You need summaries within seconds of the meeting ending, not 10 minutes later when you're already in the next call.
- **Data control and privacy.** Some organizations can't send conversations to the cloud. Others don't care. Understanding where your data goes and who processes it matters before you commit.
- **Actually useful AI, not just AI for the sake of it.** The summary should distill the key points, not merely reiterate the transcript in bullet points.

Now let's look at how each tool handles summary generation.
